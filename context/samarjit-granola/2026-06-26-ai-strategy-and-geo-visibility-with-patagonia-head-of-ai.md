## Notes

### Patagonia’s AI Strategy

- Head of AI role is relatively new, building a distributed AI team serving all business units
- Tech team acts as an internal enabler; marketing has its own digital/ecom team with separate objectives
- Core brand constraint: prioritize human connection, avoid replacing people with automation
  - No customer-facing chatbots, no app, onshore customer service with direct human pickup
  - This shapes all AI deployment decisions


### GEO and Marketing Priorities

- GEO is a top priority for the AI team this fiscal year (started last month)
- Attribution remains the central challenge: AI-sourced DTC traffic is low and likely undercounted
  - Hard to tie GEO investment directly to conversions, similar to billboard attribution

- Current GEO stack is a mixed bag: Salesforce Commerce Cloud for product catalog, Microsoft D365 for product data, Bloomreach for product-to-query mapping
- Third-party GEO providers in use but names not recalled; strategy still emerging
- AI use cases already live: LLM-powered search query mapping to improve on-site results, translation

### Customer Engagement and Product Feedback

- Pro network ([patagonia.com/pro](http://patagonia.com/pro)) is the main ambassador/sampling channel
  - Eligibility via pay stub or membership card: ski instructors, river guides, wildland fire crews, etc.
  - Field testers take unreleased gear on expeditions and return detailed product feedback

- Reviews processed internally for product bug identification; some AI-powered summarization (similar to Amazon’s review digest)
- Agent-assisted customer service is a near-term AI opportunity
  - Real-time transcription, sentiment extraction, and surfacing full customer history during calls
  - Goal: eliminate repetitive questions by leveraging existing purchase and browsing data


### Next Steps

- **Explore Patagonia internship program**
  Head of AI is restarting the internship program; an opportunity to gain inside knowledge relevant to the startup.
- **Stay in touch as GEO attribution grows**
  Patagonia's GEO spend will increase as attribution numbers move from single digits to more meaningful levels.
