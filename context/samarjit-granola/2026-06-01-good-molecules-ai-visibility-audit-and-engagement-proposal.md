### Good Molecules AI Audit Presentation

- Custom-built platform missing crucial AI crawler infrastructure
  - No robots.txt, llms.txt, llms-full.txt files
  - Missing [agents.md](http://agents.md), well-known UCP, MCP endpoint protocols
  - AI assistants (Claude, Bing Copilot) blocked from reading site or hallucinating

- Current strengths identified
  - Strong online sentiment across Reddit, Trustpilot, BBB, YouTube
  - Good editorial coverage and reviews
  - Solid foundation for AI optimization improvements
- Technical findings
  - Price confusion between product sizes (12ml vs 30ml serums)
  - Target/Ulta listings appearing instead of direct site in AI responses
  - Brand page blocked preventing detailed product information access
