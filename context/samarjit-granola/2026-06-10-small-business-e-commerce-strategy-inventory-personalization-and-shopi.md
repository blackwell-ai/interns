## Notes

### Fashionology Conference Takeaways

- Virtual fitting rooms stood out, especially for pop-up/outdoor market use cases
  - Customers unlikely to use at-home virtual try-on, but in-person demos could replace portable fitting rooms
- Styling suggestion tools promising but not quite there yet
  - Ideal: input an event/scenario, get shoppable outfit suggestions
- Omnichannel continuity a key theme: online purchases returnable in-store seamlessly
- Personalization as a driver of targeted shopping experiences

### AI in Customer-Facing vs. Back-Office Roles

- Clear distinction drawn between transactional and relationship-building interactions
  - Transactional (order status, return policy): fine to automate
  - Relationship-building: human touch preferred and seen as a competitive differentiator vs. Amazon
- Examples of high-touch moments worth keeping human:
  - In-person styling and sizing advice
  - Special orders for out-of-stock colors
  - Pairing recommendations for petite or specific-fit customers

### Inventory and Cash Flow Management

- Seasonal inventory planning is a core operational challenge
  - Example: wool fingerless gloves ordered in July, sold in November/December
  - Cash tied up for months before revenue returns
- Current toolstack: spreadsheets, QuickBooks, Shopify historical data, Settle (purchase order/payment software)
  - Also works with a bookkeeper for financial health oversight
- Visibility into reorder timing and cash-on-hand is the key need

### Shopify Pain Points

- App ecosystem costs add up fast for small businesses
  - Self-serve returns, product filtering, and lead-gen quiz all required paid third-party apps
  - Lead-gen quiz was especially hard to find: needed shoppable results page at end, not just a quiz flow
- Prefers Shopify-approved apps over custom dev for security and convenience
  - Wary of how customer data is handled by third-party contractors
- Most apps are subscription-based; currently on free tiers where volume allows

### Next Steps

- Build out inventory management and lead-time planning tool
- Send a demo video once built; schedule follow-up call if there’s interest
