### Core Pain Point: Invisible Upstream Context

- Profound only sees when Sweetwater is cited, not the conversation that led there
- Users give AI rich, specific context (e.g., “I’m a sweaty DJ in Miami, I need headphones that don’t fold”) that Sweetwater never sees
- Without that context, it’s impossible to know what questions to answer proactively
- Likely outcome: AI platforms will build their own shopping integrations and own this context layer entirely

### Funnel Strategy Shift and New KPIs Needed

- Traditional SEO is bottom-of-funnel: click-through rate, bounce rate, time on page, conversion
- AI is pulling engagement to top and mid funnel: users who don’t yet know they want to buy
- Content strategy must shift: articles and videos targeting people earlier in their journey
- Videos attached to articles get served directly by AI, creating branding and subscriber opportunities
- EEAT algorithm (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trustworthiness) now drives whose answer gets served
- Whoever maps brand-lane metrics (sentiment, page lift) to AI mid/top-funnel performance will win
  - Most competitors are still focused on zero-click loss and bottom-of-funnel traffic decline
