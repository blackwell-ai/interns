## Notes

### Mozi Wash AI Visibility Audit Findings

- Only cited once by Gemini; citation didn’t repeat the next day
- Blog posts are the one positive signal LLMs pick up
- Most third-party review sites carry mixed or negative signals
- Okendo reviews not readable by LLMs
  - Schema supports aggregate rating and review tags, but not yet populated
- Laundry Sauce (main competitor) outperforms in AI citations
  - Best fix: get publications to explicitly compare and favor Mozi Wash
- YouTube content about Mozi Wash is mostly sponsored, which LLMs discount

### Tactics Ian’s Team Proposed

- Fix Okendo schema to surface reviews in product structured data
- Third-party blog syndication
  - Repurpose existing blog posts onto journaling/content sites without Mozi’s name as publisher
  - More credible to LLMs than self-published content
- YouTube UGC: encourage creators to post organically on their own accounts
- Reddit strategy (still developing)
  - Draft replies to negative comments to address them safely
  - Incentivize existing customers with Reddit accounts to post positively
  - Recruit neutral reviewers to evaluate the product

- Blog syndication and Reddit were the two tactics that caught his interest
