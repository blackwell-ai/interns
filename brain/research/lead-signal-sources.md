manager" thesis. A multi-location Shopify retailer (Austin and San Antonio) wants

## Standing monitors (job boards)

Upwork is the only one of the four boards worth a recurring automated sweep: its
public job index exposes title, budget, post date, and duration without login
(individual pages are gated). Sort by newest and watch these saved searches.

- Inventory and AI ops (priority, closest to the current direction):
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=shopify%20inventory%20forecasting&sort=recency
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=AI%20agent%20ecommerce%20operations&sort=recency
  - Qualifies: a merchant (not an agency) wanting demand forecasting, stock
    alerts, reorder or transfer logic, dead-stock analysis, or an AI assistant to
    run parts of store operations.
- GEO and AI visibility (current revenue motion, DTC brands are warmest):
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=generative%20engine%20optimization&sort=recency
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=AI%20visibility%20ChatGPT&sort=recency
  - Budgets seen this run: $1,200 to $2,500 fixed.
- Shopify build and migration (prioritize migrations and strategic redesigns over
  cheap theme tweaks):
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=shopify%20migration&sort=recency
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=shopify%20store%20setup&sort=recency
- Local online ordering (brick-and-mortar going online):
  - https://www.upwork.com/nx/search/jobs/?q=online%20ordering%20restaurant&sort=recency

Disqualifiers on Upwork: posters who are themselves agencies or resellers,
sub-$100 one-bug tasks, dropshipping-course spam.

Craigslist computer-gigs is a secondary, locally useful stream (real buyers,
SF-local, good for the in-person batch motion):
https://sfbay.craigslist.org/search/cpg (append `?query=shopify`, `website`,
`online%20store`, or `inventory`). The web-design services section is sellers,
not buyers; skip it.

## In-person venue calendars (Bay Area)

Recurring farmers and maker markets put you in front of vendor-owners working
their own booths, the highest hit-rate for cold in-person discovery. Best
always-current calendars:

- SF night markets: https://sf.funcheap.com/city-guide/night-market-san-francisco-bay-area/
- SF farmers markets: https://sf.funcheap.com/city-guide/funcheaps-guide-farmers-markets/ and https://foodwise.org/markets/
- Bay Area maker and craft markets: https://www.eddies-list.com/p/san-francisco-bay-area-maker-craft-markets
- South Bay makers: https://www.sanjosemade.com/pages/events ; Santa Clara County markets: https://ag.santaclaracounty.gov/farmersmarket
- DTC-merchant meetups: https://www.meetup.com/Shopify-San-Francisco-Community/ and https://www.meetup.com/bay_area_amazon_shopify_sellers/

Mission and Valencia walk surface: the Valencia Corridor (16th to 24th) has the
densest cluster of independent owner-operated shops. The Valencia Corridor
Merchants Association directory (https://www.visitvalenciastreet.com) plus a
"Powered by Shopify" footer check are fast ways to pre-qualify before a walk-in.
