# Review platforms — Bazaarvoice / Influenster weaknesses

Filed June 10, 2026. Source: Granola notes from "15 min with John", May 13,
2026 — John Robeson, Anthropic startups division (johnr@anthropic.com). Call
was during the Giftly era (pre-Blackwell rebrand); YC application was the
stated priority.

## Competitive observations

- **Bazaarvoice**: reviews only come after purchase — same staleness problem as
  Amazon reviews. Building its own shopping agent ("Harmony AI")
- **Influenster**: gives free products with no obligation to review → skews to
  only-positive reviews, no negative signal
- Structural gap: people only review when they love or hate a product
- Selling data will become commoditized; a Google-AdWords-replacement strategy
  was discussed with skepticism
