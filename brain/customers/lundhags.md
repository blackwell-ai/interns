# Lundhags

Outdoor brand (sells wholesale through retailers incl. Dick's Sporting Goods).
Filed June 10, 2026. Source: Granola notes from the May 28, 2026 discovery call
("Inventory management challenges"). Granola transcribed the brand name as
"Loontogs"; Armaan identifies the call as Lundhags.

## Their problems (from the call)

### Inventory / demand planning (primary pain)

- Predicting demand across product variations (colors, sizes) is the core
  challenge
- Current process: Excel models taking **2–3 days of work per planning cycle**
- Minimum order quantity (MOQ) constraints force iterative planning: remove
  low-demand variants (e.g. gray), redistribute demand to remaining options
  (blue, black)
- **Critical data gap: no sell-through data from retailers** like Dick's
  Sporting Goods — they see wholesale orders, not actual consumer sales, so
  true demand patterns are invisible

### Marketing & brand awareness

- Most marketing outsourced to agencies (size constraints); agencies use
  proprietary tools, so the brand has limited first-hand platform knowledge
- Uses wholesale distribution partly for brand exposure
- Interested in optimizing ROAS and increasing reach

## What Blackwell pitched

- AI visibility: website optimization (robots.txt, llms.txt, enhanced sitemaps,
  schema markup) + sentiment campaigns (Reddit mentions, backlinks for answer
  engines); no separate bot-focused site needed
- Small brands can move faster than large ones in AI optimization
- Referenced prior audits: Public Goods, Chariot Energy
