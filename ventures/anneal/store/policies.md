# Store policies, draft

Print-on-demand appropriate. These are drafts written to be accurate to a made-on-order model. Have a human read them before they go live, since they are a legal surface. They are not legal advice.

## Shipping

Every item is printed on order. Production takes two to four business days, then shipping takes another two to five within the US. International varies by destination.

We do not hold stock, so we cannot offer same-day or overnight. Tracking is emailed when the order ships.

## Returns and exchanges

Because each item is made for you when you order, we cannot accept returns for change of mind. We do replace anything that arrives flawed, misprinted, or damaged.

If something is wrong, email [support address] within 30 days with your order number and a photo. We reprint or refund, your choice, and you do not need to ship the flawed item back.

Size matters more here than usual since exchanges are limited, so the size guide on each product is accurate to the blank. When in doubt, the fit notes tell you whether to size up.

## Privacy

We collect only what an order needs: your name, shipping address, email, and the payment details handled by our processor. We use them to make and ship your order and to email you about it. We do not sell your data. You can ask us to delete your data at any time at [support address].

Payments are processed by the platform's payment provider. We never see or store full card numbers.

## Terms of sale

Prices are in US dollars and can change. We may cancel and refund an order if a product is mispriced or unavailable from our fulfillment partner. Designs and brand marks are owned by the brand and not for resale or reproduction.

## Product safety and labeling note (internal, not for the storefront)

Apparel sold in the US carries labeling obligations under the FTC textile and care-labeling rules: fiber content, country of origin, and care instructions. Print-on-demand partners apply the blank manufacturer's labels, which covers most of this, but confirm at setup that the partner's labels meet the requirement for the blanks we pick. This is one of the items a human should verify in the setup session.
