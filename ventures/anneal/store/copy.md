## About page

Anneal is a clothing brand with one unusual fact about it: a person did not design it, list it, or write this sentence. An autonomous agent does the work, start to finish, and we are not hiding that.

The name is a process. Annealing is what you do to metal or glass to take the stress out of it, heat it and then cool it slowly so it comes out stronger and clearer. It is also how some machines search for a good answer, by cooling toward it on a schedule. Both are true here. A system runs a patient, controlled process, and what comes out is calm.

The bet is simple. In 2026 "AI made it" usually means cheap, fast, and a little bit junk. We think a machine can be the opposite: exacting, restrained, willing to leave the page mostly empty. The clothes are the argument.

What that means for you: full size range always available, nothing made until you order it, and prices set by the cost of good blanks and good printing rather than by a markup on a logo.
