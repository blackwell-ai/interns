## Comparison summary (2026-06-29)
================================================================
All four brands are on Shopify with the native UCP agentic layer (/agents.md,
/.well-known/ucp = 200) and full AI-bot crawlability. Structured data is the
separator only at the margin:
- Raw Elements: richest homepage entity — JSON-LD carries Organization, Brand,
  Product, Offer, AggregateRating, ContactPoint, PostalAddress. Strongest
  machine-readable identity of the set.
- Stream2Sea, Badger, Thinksport: basic homepage entity (Organization + WebSite +
  SearchAction); product pages carry Product + Offer (+ Brand for S2S/Thinksport).
Conclusion: on-site plumbing does not separate these brands. The AI-visibility gap
is Recommendability (live citations) and Reputation (off-site), where Project Reef
and Badger lead and Stream2Sea trails.
